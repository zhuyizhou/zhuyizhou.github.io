New Publication in ACS Appl. Energy Mater.

We are pleased to announce that our latest research paper titled "Elucidating Anisotropic Ionic Diffusion Mechanism in Li3YCl6 with Molecular Dynamics Simulations" has been published in ACS Appl. Energy Mater.

Research Highlights

  • Revealed the anisotropic nature of lithium-ion diffusion in Li3YCl6
  • Identified preferred diffusion pathways using molecular dynamics simulations
  • Provided insights for optimizing halide solid electrolytes

This work represents a significant step forward in understanding the fundamental mechanisms of ion transport in halide solid electrolytes, which are promising candidates for next-generation all-solid-state batteries.

Publication Details

Authors: Jiazhong Geng, Zihan Yan, Yizhou Zhu*

Journal: ACS Applied Energy Materials

DOI: 10.1021/acsaem.4c01249
