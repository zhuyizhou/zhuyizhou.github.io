Sep 1, 2023

New Member Joining

We are delighted to welcome Zihan Yan to our research group as a Ph.D. student. Zihan received his B.S. degree from Yangzhou University, where he demonstrated excellent academic performance and research potential.

Research Focus

At the Computational Materials Lab, Zihan will focus on:

  • Development of machine learning potentials for solid electrolytes
  • Investigation of lithium ion transport mechanisms using advanced simulation techniques
  • Design and optimization of novel solid-state electrolyte materials

His research will contribute to our ongoing efforts in understanding and improving solid-state battery materials through computational approaches.
